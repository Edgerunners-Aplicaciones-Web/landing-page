Our Products: Technology that Transforms Hotel Management

From reservations to smart experiences, all in one place.

Download Brochure

Smart Stay offers a modular ecosystem, divided into key areas of hotel management. You can choose exactly what your hotel needs, or integrate them all to get a complete solution.

Hotel Management

  • PMS (Property Management System): Control of reservations, rooms, check-in/out.
  • Channel Manager: Synchronization with Booking, Expedia, Airbnb.
  • Web Booking Engine: Direct reservations from the hotel's website.

Guest Experience

  • Smart Room Control (IoT): Control of lights, air, TV from mobile.
  • App for Guests: Online check-in, room service, requests.
  • Feedback and Surveys: Real-time opinions with AI analytics.

Financial & Operational

  • Financial Reports: Dashboard with revenue and expense metrics.
  • Payment Management: Digital cards, e-wallets, electronic invoicing.
  • Inventory and Housekeeping: Supply management, cleaning, maintenance.

Security & Support

  • Access Control: Digital or NFC keys for guests and staff.
  • 24/7 Support & Training: Assistance for staff and continuous training.
